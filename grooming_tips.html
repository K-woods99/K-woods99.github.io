Cat Grooming Tips

Learn how to keep your cat clean and healthy with these grooming tips.

Bathing Your Cat

Schedule baths when your cat is calm. Trim claws, brush fur, protect ears, use lukewarm water and cat shampoo, rinse thoroughly, dry gently.

Brushing Your Cat

Regular brushing removes dirt, grease, and dead hair. Check coat condition, use metal comb for short-haired cats, comb upward for long-haired cats, check for skin problems and pests.

Skin Problems

Watch for symptoms like scratching, scabs, redness. Causes include parasites, allergies, infections, and stress. Consult vet for diagnosis and treatment.

Shedding

Shedding is normal, but excessive shedding can signal health issues like allergies or infections. Maintain a healthy diet, groom regularly, and monitor for abnormalities.
